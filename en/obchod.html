I offer original Japanese calligraphy for sale.
I create my calligraphy using traditional Japanese technique, with respect for the quality of the materials and the energy of the moment in which the stroke is made. I work with high-pigment inks and brushes made from natural fibres, which allow me to capture the breath of the moment. I take water for mixing ink from natural springs — not chemically treated — to keep each stroke as pure and honest as possible.

My aim is not decoration, but truth. The character must carry the power of its meaning — not just the beauty of its shape.

Each piece is one of a kind, created in silence and full presence.

As the masters in Japan teach — one stroke, one thought. One character, one universe.
