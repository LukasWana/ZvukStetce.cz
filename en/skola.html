IROHA SCHOOL

I founded the IROHA calligraphy school in Prague, which has around fifty students. Some of them I prepare for examinations at the Japanese calligraphy school in Tokyo.

I teach in the traditional rinsho method, through copying texts of old masters and templates from basic style to abstract. At the same time, I guide my students towards creative and distinctive writing.
